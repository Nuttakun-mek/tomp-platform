# Driver Card Specification

Driver Card is the main driver interface.

## Design Principle

Driver must not navigate complex menus. Driver should see one clear card with current and next action.

## Driver Card Must Answer

1. What is my call sign?
2. What is my current mission?
3. Where do I go?
4. What time must I arrive?
5. Who do I call?
6. What is next?
7. What do I press if there is a problem?

## Main Sections

### Header

- Project name
- Operation day
- Call sign
- Status

### Current Assignment

- Mission name
- Pickup location
- Drop-off location
- Commitment time
- Instruction

### Quick Actions

- Call coordinator
- Call operation
- Open Google Maps
- Update status
- Report issue

### Route Sheet

Timeline of today's assigned movements.

### Issue Buttons

- Traffic
- Cannot find pickup point
- Passenger not found
- Vehicle problem
- Need coordinator
- Change reported by organizer
- Other

## Activation Checklist

- Confirm driver name
- Confirm phone
- Confirm vehicle
- Take vehicle photo
- Take plate photo
- Enable GPS
- Confirm route sheet
- Confirm ready
