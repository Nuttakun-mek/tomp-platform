# Service Catalog

TOMP must support multiple transportation service types.

## Core Services

### Airport Pickup

Transportation from airport to hotel or event venue.

Key needs:

- Flight or arrival reference
- Pickup point
- Coordinator contact
- Passenger group
- Vehicle readiness
- Driver instruction
- Google Maps link
- Arrival confirmation

### Airport Drop-off

Transportation from hotel or venue to airport.

Key needs:

- Pickup schedule
- Passenger readiness
- Drop-off terminal
- Buffer time
- Luggage consideration

### Hotel Transfer

Transportation between hotel and event venue.

Key needs:

- Hotel lobby point
- Waiting zone
- Passenger group
- Time window
- Coordinator confirmation

### Conference Shuttle

Repeated shuttle movements between venues.

Key needs:

- Rotation schedule
- Holding area
- Vehicle capacity
- Coordinator checkpoints
- Status board

### VIP Transfer

High-priority transportation for important passengers.

Key needs:

- Higher buffer
- Dedicated coordinator
- Priority contact
- Special vehicle requirement
- Restricted visibility
- Faster escalation

### Factory Visit / Site Visit

Transportation to offsite venue.

Key needs:

- Route instruction
- Parking instruction
- Site contact
- Safety note
- Group coordination

### Dinner Transfer

Transportation to restaurant or dinner venue.

Key needs:

- Time flexibility
- Coordinator confirmation
- Holding area
- Return plan

### Standby

Vehicle and driver wait at defined location ready for dispatch.

Key needs:

- Holding location
- Standby window
- Response time
- Coordinator contact
- Release condition

### Emergency Transport

Unplanned or urgent movement.

Key needs:

- Fast assignment
- Operation approval
- Incident link
- Recovery tracking
