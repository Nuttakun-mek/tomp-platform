## Project

Main container for operational work.

## Operation Day

A specific date of operation inside a project.

## Session

A logical activity period inside an operation day.

## Mission

A service activity to be delivered.

## Assignment

Allocation of mission to driver, vehicle, call sign, and time window.

## Commitment

The service promise: target time, target location, service level, and success criteria.

## Call Sign

Operational identity used in communication.

## Vehicle

Transportation resource.

## Driver

Person operating vehicle.

## Coordinator

Field person who confirms arrival, boarding, completion, and issues.

## Organizer

External or customer-side project partner.

## Timeline Event

Immutable record of what happened.

## Incident

A problem or abnormal event requiring tracking.

## Recovery

Action taken to restore service commitment.

## Notification

Actionable message sent to relevant user.

## Venue

Operational location with pickup, drop-off, parking, holding area, contact, and map links.
