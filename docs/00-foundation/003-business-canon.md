# Business Canon

Business Canon defines permanent business truths for TOMP.

## Canon

1. The company sells service assurance, not only vehicles.
2. Project is the main container of operational work.
3. Mission is the service activity.
4. Assignment is the allocation of resources to a mission.
5. Call Sign is operational identity, not vehicle plate and not driver name.
6. Vehicle can change while Call Sign remains.
7. Driver can change while Assignment history remains.
8. Published Plan is the operational baseline.
9. After Publish, changes must be recorded as Change Events.
10. Every significant action must be captured in Timeline.
11. GPS supports operations but does not define truth alone.
12. Google Maps is for navigation; TOMP is for operations management.
13. Driver Portal must be simple, action-oriented, and mobile-first.
14. Operation workspace must highlight exception and risk.
15. Knowledge must belong to the company, not individual staff.
16. QR access is essential for temporary drivers and coordinators.
17. Multi-role access is required because one person may serve multiple project roles.
18. Organizer can be viewer, collaborator, or change requester based on project settings.
19. System must support manual override with reason and audit.
20. Zero-cost pilot is preferred until business scale justifies paid infrastructure.
